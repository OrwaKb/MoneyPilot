MoneyPilot — your personal finance cockpit
===========================================

EASIEST: run the installer
  Run  MoneyPilot-Setup.exe  and click through it. It installs MoneyPilot for
  you (no admin needed), adds a Start-menu shortcut, and you're done. This is
  the recommended way.

  (Windows may show "Windows protected your PC" because the app isn't paid-code-
  signed. Click "More info" -> "Run anyway". It's safe.)

USING THE ZIP INSTEAD?
  If you have MoneyPilot-windows.zip rather than the installer, unzip it to a
  PLAIN LOCAL FOLDER such as  C:\MoneyPilot
  *** Do NOT run it from inside OneDrive or your Desktop ***  — OneDrive turns
  files into cloud placeholders and the app fails to start there. Then run
  MoneyPilot.exe from C:\MoneyPilot.

FIRST RUN
  A quick setup walks you through salary, payday, and your current balance.
  Your data stays on YOUR computer (C:\Users\<you>\AppData\Local\MoneyPilot) and
  is never uploaded. Daily backups are kept beside it automatically.

THE AI ADVISOR (optional)
  The chat advisor, the daily briefing, and smart auto-categorizing use Claude
  and need your OWN Claude account (Pro/Max). Open the ADVISOR tab and click
  "Connect AI" to sign in. Without it, the AI just shows "not connected" and
  EVERYTHING ELSE still works — tracking, budgets, the daily allowance, goals,
  and CSV export.

NOTES
  - Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit).
  - If the window is blank on first launch, install Microsoft's free
    "WebView2 Runtime" (search that name), then reopen.
