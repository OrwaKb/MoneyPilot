MoneyPilot — your personal finance cockpit
===========================================

GETTING STARTED
  1) Unzip this whole folder somewhere you'll keep it (e.g. your Desktop).
  2) Double-click  MoneyPilot.exe  to start.
  3) The first run walks you through a quick setup (salary, payday, balance).

Your data stays on YOUR computer, in:
  C:\Users\<you>\AppData\Local\MoneyPilot
Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Daily backups are saved next to it automatically.


THE AI ADVISOR (optional)
  The chat advisor, the daily briefing, and smart auto-categorizing of what you
  type use Claude. They need your OWN Claude account:
    - Open the ADVISOR tab and click "Connect AI".
    - A sign-in window opens — sign in with your Claude account, then come back
      and click "re-check".
  This works with a Claude Pro or Max subscription. Without it, the AI just
  shows "not connected" and EVERYTHING ELSE still works — tracking spending,
  budgets, the rolling daily allowance, goals, and CSV export.


NOTES
  - Windows 10 or 11.
  - If the window is blank on first launch, install Microsoft's free
    "WebView2 Runtime" (search that name), then reopen MoneyPilot.
  - The first start can take a few seconds while it warms up.
